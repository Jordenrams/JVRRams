Music is more than just a melody! There is a story behind every song. It should inspire people, but it also has a story. One song is about love, while another is about hate. The message of a song is often deep and not always explained by the artist to his listeners.

In the story of the song, it becomes clear why the artist made the song. How he felt about it and what his thoughts were. There is a chance that the listener will not agree. Wiki of Somalia; Somalia , is a country in the Horn of Africa, bordering Djibouti, Ethiopia and Kenya. The capital of Somalia is Mogadishu. Somali and Arabic are the official languages of Somalia. With a coastline of 3000 kilometres, Somalia has the longest coastline of any country on the African continent and in the Arab world. Because Somalia is culturally, historically, ethnically, linguistically, geographically and religiously linked to the Arab world, it is a member of the Arab League. The city has fallen. What am I to do now?

Wikipedia of Lothal; Lothal (Gujarat) (Hill of the Dead) was one of the most important cities of the ancient Indus civilisation. It was located in the modern state of Gujarat and was founded around 2400 BC. It is the most important archaeological site in India of this civilisation. Lothal was discovered in 1954 and excavations by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) began on 13 February 1955 and continued until 19 May 1960. Eventually, the constant threats of tropical storms and floods caused so much damage that the city was disrupted and eventually destroyed. The cause for the abandonment of the city may therefore have been a change of climate in addition to the natural disasters.
